Love for music

I've grown up around an environment where there was always music or some form of it. The family I grew up were artist's themselves my father along with his brothers had a group named KLS they were one of the first Hmong groups in America and they continued to set a foundation to the sound of Hmong music so that it was on par with the modern sound.

They ignited my love for music as I surrounded myself around them throughout my entire childhood. Growing up I was always listening to their albums.
